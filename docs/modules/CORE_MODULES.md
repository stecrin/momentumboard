# MomentumBoard — Core Modules

## 1. Recovery Dashboard

The first screen of the system.

Purpose:
- show reality clearly
- reduce confusion
- show only what matters today

Displays:
- today’s recovery status
- job applications progress
- money pressure
- urgent actions
- emotional boundary reminder
- daily minimum standard

---

## 2. Daily Stabilisation Engine

Purpose:
Build the day based on current state, not fantasy energy.

Inputs:
- energy level
- stress level
- money pressure
- sleep quality
- demanding periods
- urgent deadlines

Outputs:
- 1 major mission
- 2 support actions
- 3 non-negotiables

---

## 3. Money Pressure Panel

Purpose:
Face financial reality without shame.

Tracks:
- rent arrears
- debts
- cash available
- expected income
- urgent bills
- assets to sell

Rule:
Money stress must become visible, simple, and actionable.

---

## 4. Job Recovery Engine

Purpose:
Create income stability.

Tracks:
- daily job applications
- target salary
- roles applied for
- CV versions
- follow-ups
- interviews

Daily rule:
Minimum 3 job applications per day.

---

## 5. Emotional Boundary Tracker

Purpose:
Protect mental clarity from emotional destabilisation.

Tracks:
- emotional triggers
- conflict loops avoided
- calm responses
- boundaries respected
- moments where focus was protected

This is not about blaming anyone.
It is about protecting execution.

---

## 6. Emergency Reset Mode

Purpose:
When overloaded, reduce life to the next safe action.

Sequence:
1. Stop
2. Drink water
3. Breathe for 60 seconds
4. Write only 3 tasks
5. Pick the smallest one
6. Work for 10 minutes

---

## 7. Weekly Recovery Review

Purpose:
Review reality and adjust strategy.

Questions:
- What did I complete?
- What did I avoid?
- What caused stress?
- What created stability?
- What must be removed next week?
- What is the main mission for next week?
