Stefan Command System — Quick Guide

How to Use

Your complete guide to the Stefan Command System.

Quick Start

When you feel lost or overwhelmed, run this sequence

1
Open Inbox — dump everything from your head

Type all tasks, thoughts, and worries in one go. Messy is fine. Commas or new lines separate them. Do not filter or judge yet.

2
Click "✦ Organise My Brain"

The system analyses every inbox item and suggests category, priority, energy level, and the next smallest action automatically.

3
Review suggestions — Accept, Edit, or Skip

Agree with most? Click "Accept All → Tasks". Need to adjust one? Click Edit. Don't agree with something? Click Skip — it stays in inbox.

4
Go to Daily 3 — click "✦ Build Today For Me"

The system picks your Major Mission from the highest priority tasks, 2 Medium Tasks, and 3 Non-Negotiables automatically. It respects your Brain State.

5
Start your Major Mission immediately

Do not reorganise. Do not re-read everything. The plan is set — execute it. Everything else waits.

Daily Workflow

How to run each day with the system

Morning 10–15 min
  • Brain dump everything on your mind into Inbox
  • Click "Organise My Brain" and accept tasks
  • Go to Daily 3 → "Build Today For Me"
  • Start the Major Mission immediately — do not wait
During the Day
  • New thought hits? Add to Inbox only — do not act on it yet
  • Do not re-plan or reorganise mid-session
  • Tick tasks off as you complete them
  • Feeling overwhelmed? Press "I feel lost"
Evening 5 min
  • Mark remaining tasks: done or deferred?
  • Write 3 sentences in Weekly Review
  • Glance at tomorrow — anything truly urgent?
  • Close the system. Rest.
Feature Guide

What each feature does

📥
Brain Dump Inbox

Dump all thoughts here — messy is fine. Separate by commas or new lines. This is your input buffer, not your task manager. Nothing in inbox is a commitment yet.

✦
Organise My Brain

Analyses every inbox item using keyword rules. Detects the right category, assigns priority, estimates energy needed, and suggests the next smallest action. Review and accept.

📋
Task Breakdown

Deep-dive on any single task: define the final outcome, the smallest first step, urgency, money impact, stress reduction, deadline, and energy required. A task without a next action is not actionable.

⚡
Priority Engine

Scores tasks automatically: Urgent +3 • Makes Money +2 • Reduces Stress +1 • Deadline +1 to +3. Score ≥4 = High Priority. Score ≥2 = Medium. Below = Low.

📌
Daily 3

Your only command for today: 1 Major Mission, 2 Medium Tasks, 3 Non-Negotiables. If it is not on this list, it does not exist today. Build it once each morning, then execute it.

🧠
Brain State Modes

Tell the system how you feel right now. It adjusts which tasks are surfaced, how the Daily 3 is built, and what is dimmed or highlighted to match your current capacity.

📅
Weekly Planner

Assign tasks to specific days of the week. See the full week at a glance. Print it for your desk or wall. Today's column is always highlighted.

✦
Auto Plan Week

Distributes all tasks intelligently: high priority tasks early in the week, spiritual tasks on Sunday, health tasks on Saturday, maximum 3 tasks per day. Adjusts with one click.

🔍
Weekly Review

End-of-week debrief. What did you complete? What did you avoid? What caused stress? What is the single priority for next week? Builds self-awareness over time.

🆘
Emergency Reset

Press "I feel lost" when overwhelmed. The system guides you: stop, drink water, breathe for 60 seconds, write only 3 tasks, choose the smallest one, start for 10 minutes only.

Brain State Modes

Match the system to your energy and situation

🟢
Normal

Default mode. Full task list visible. All priorities shown normally. Use for regular planning and execution days.

🔵
Low Energy

When tired, unwell, or low motivation. High-energy tasks are visually dimmed. The system surfaces lighter work you can actually complete today.

🔴
Emergency

Everything feels on fire. Only survival, money, and urgent admin tasks are highlighted. Everything else fades out. Triage first, everything else later.

🌑
Deep Focus

One task only. A fullscreen overlay shows your Major Mission and its next action. All distractions are hidden. Work in timed blocks without interruption.

Rules of the System

These rules protect your attention and momentum

1

Do not fight more than one major battle per day. One major mission. Everything else supports it or waits until tomorrow.

2

If everything feels urgent, use Emergency Reset. Urgency is often a feeling, not a fact. Reset before you react. Breathe first.

3

Money and survival tasks come before cosmetic tasks. Rent, income, bills, and urgent admin are never optional. Decoration and nice-to-haves are.

4

A task is not ready until it has a smallest next action. "Fix the car" is not a task. "Call the garage to book an appointment" is a task.

5

Daily 3 is the command for the day. Build it once in the morning. Execute it. Do not rebuild or re-plan mid-day — it kills momentum.

6

Everything else waits. Inbox items are not tasks yet. They wait until you decide they matter enough to plan. The inbox holds them safely.

Recurring Tasks

Set up tasks that repeat automatically

1
Open Task Breakdown for any task

Click "Break Down" from the Inbox, or "Edit" on any existing task in the Tasks tab. Scroll to the "Recurring Task" section at the bottom.

2
Check "Repeat this task" and choose a frequency

Daily (every day), Weekdays (Mon–Fri), Weekly (pick one day), or Custom (pick any combination). For Weekly and Custom, tick the specific days.

3
Recurring tasks appear in Daily 3 suggestions automatically

On the correct days, the task surfaces in your suggestion pool and in "Build Today For Me". It shows a 🔁 badge with its schedule.

4
Tick it done — it resets the next day

Recurring tasks use daily completion tracking. Ticking one done marks it for today only — it dims and moves below active tasks. Tomorrow it reappears fresh. It never permanently disappears.

5
Pause with ⏸ — resume with ▶

Use pause when you need to skip a period without deleting the task. A paused task shows ⏸ Paused and won't appear in Daily 3 until resumed.

Data Backup

Keep your data safe with export and import

1
Click “↓ Backup” in the header

Downloads a file named stefan-command-system-backup-YYYY-MM-DD.json with all your tasks, daily plan, weekly plan, and reviews. Do this regularly.

2
Click “↑ Import” to restore from a backup

Select a previously downloaded JSON backup file. All current data will be replaced. A confirmation prompt appears before anything is overwritten.

3
Run from localhost for reliable storage

Opening index.html directly uses file:// which some browsers clear on restart. For reliable data: open a terminal in the project folder and run python3 -m http.server 8080, then open http://localhost:8080 in your browser. A yellow warning appears if you are on file://.

Subtasks

Break any task into small, trackable steps

1
Open Task Breakdown for any task

Click “Break Down” from the Inbox, or “Edit” from the Tasks tab. Scroll to the Subtasks section at the bottom of the modal.

2
Click “✦ Suggest Subtasks” for automatic steps

The system detects the task type and suggests relevant subtasks — car paint, Student Finance, UC account check, YouTube video, LinkedIn, gym, and more. A generic fallback is used for everything else.

3
Add your own with “+ Add Subtask”

Each subtask has a description, a checkbox, and an optional time estimate. Edit or remove subtasks freely before saving.

4
Tick subtasks off as you work

Open the task breakdown at any time to tick individual subtasks. Every task card shows progress — for example “2/5 subtasks complete”. When all subtasks are done the progress indicator turns green.
